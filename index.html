Who We Are

Built on Reliability,
Driven by Excellence

Royal Carriers Inc. is a family-owned, owner-operated freight company with over nine years of consistent performance in the OTR trucking industry. Based in Texas, we run late-model equipment and take pride in treating every load like it's our own.

We specialize in dry van and refrigerated freight, connecting shippers and brokers to dependable capacity across the continental United States. Our drivers are experienced, safety-conscious professionals who represent our brand on every mile.

We've grown from the ground up — no outside capital, no shortcuts. Just a Texas family that knows freight and delivers on its word.
