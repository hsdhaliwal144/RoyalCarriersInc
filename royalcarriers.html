Who We Are

Built on Reliability,
Driven by Excellence

Royal Carriers Inc. is a family-owned, owner-operated freight company with over five years of consistent performance in the OTR trucking industry. We run a tight, professional fleet and take pride in treating every load like it's our own.

We specialize in dry van and refrigerated freight, connecting shippers and brokers to dependable capacity across the continental United States. Our drivers are experienced, safety-conscious professionals who represent our brand on every mile.

We've grown from the ground up — no outside capital, no shortcuts. Just excellent service and hard work.
